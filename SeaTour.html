El Nido

El Nido is a Philippine municipality on Palawan island. It's known for white-sand beaches, coral reefs and as the gateway to the Bacuit archepelago, a group of islands with steep karst cliffs.

Boracay

Boracay is a small island in the central Philippines. It's known for its resorts and beaches.

Puerto Galera

Perto Galera is a town on the Philippine island of Mindoro. It's known for its dive sites and beaches. The long curve of White Beach is backered by bars and resorts.

Samal Island

Samal, officially the Island Garden City of Samal, or sometimes called IGaCOS, is a 4th class city in the province of Davao Del Norte, Philippines.

Panglao Island

Panglao Island is an island in the north Bohol Sea, located in the Central Visayas region of the Visayas island group, in the south-central Philippines.

Coron Island

Coron is the third-largest island in the Calamian Islands in the northern Palawan in the Philippines. The island is part of the Larger municipality of the same name.

Palaui Island

The Palaui Island Protected Landscape and Seascape is a protected area and national par in the town of Santa Ana in Cagayan, Philippines. It is located off the northeastern extremity of Luzon Island, the largest island in the country.

Pagudpud Island

Pagudpud is a coastal town filled with Ilocandalia delights and adventure. Located on the northern tip of Luzon, thousands of visitors are drawn to this town annually because of its alluring coastlines and remarkable architectural attractions.

Siargao Island

Siargao is a tear-drop shaped island in the Philippine Sea situated 800 kilometers southeast of Manila in the province of Siargao Del Norte.

Mactan Island

Mactan or Maktan lies across the Mactan Channel opposite Cebu City. It is modern and yet has retained its greenness and features lovely sandy beaches and good diving spots off the neighboring islets.

Kalanggaman Island

Kalanggaman Island is one of the most beautiful islands in the Philippines. The water so clear and blue, the sands so white like powder. It looks like a smaller version of Boracay Island. What is unique about it is its magnificient sandbars, both stretching at the end of the islands.

Canigao Island

Canigao is an islet located in the Philippines, near the municipality of Matalom, Leyte. The area is known for abundant fishing grounds and scenic coral reef areas suitable for diving.

Dakak Beach

A diving mecca found in Northern Zamboanga in Mindanao. It offers visitors and diving enthusiasts with not only copious coral reefs with thriving tropical marine life, but also a sanctuary of native plants and animals..

White Beach Malamawi Island

A picturesque beach found in one of the secluded areas in the Philippines. It offers its visitors with shores that are filled with fine granules of white sand lining its pristine turquoise waters.

Limasawa Island

Limasawa, an island municipality in Southern Leyte, is a small yet flourishing town being the site of the First Christian Mass in the country and in Asia.

Mahabang Buhangin Beach

Located in the town of San Juan, the area has white sand and a living colony of corals. It lies in a cave that is a prerequisite for a fine beach. A cave nearby adds to the attraction of the beach.

Cuatro Islas

Leyte's Hidden Gems. Cuatro Islas is a group of Islands belonging to the municipalities of Inopacan and Hindang, in the province of Leyte, Philippines.

Higatangan Island

Higatangan Island is a part of Naval Municipality that is separated by a narrow channel located in the northwest tip of Leyte.

Biliran Island

Biliran is the best kept secret in Eastern Visayas. It has its charming villages, rich coastlines, rugged mountains and majestic waterfalls. The island attractions raw beauty and the thrill of discovering a new flora and fauna are what appeal to the masses.

San Pablo Island

Also known as Pong Gamay in Hinunangan, Southern Leyte. It is simply one of the most beautiful hidden getaways there are in the locality.
